What to do right now

Follow these steps immediately. Time matters.

  1. 1
    Stop all contact

    Do not send any more money. Block the scammer on all platforms.

  2. 2
    Contact your bank immediately

    Call the number on the back of your card. Ask for the fraud team.

  3. 3
    Report to Action Fraud

    Report online at actionfraud.police.uk or call 0300 123 2040.

  4. 4
    Secure your accounts

    Change passwords on any accounts that may be compromised.

  5. 5
    Get a full fraud assessment

    Use Fraud Help Index to identify your fraud type and get tailored guidance.
