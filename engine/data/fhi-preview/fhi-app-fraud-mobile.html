APP Fraud — Can You Get Your Money Back?

Under UK law, your bank is legally required to reimburse most APP fraud up to £85,000.

The short answer

Under PSR mandatory reimbursement rules — effective 7 October 2024 — UK banks must reimburse APP fraud victims up to £85,000. Say "APP fraud" when you call.

What to do right now

Every minute counts.

  1. 1
    Call your bank now

    Ask for Payment Services / Fraud team. Say "APP fraud".

  2. 2
    "APP fraud" triggers a legal protocol

    Banks must investigate and reimburse most losses up to £85,000.

  3. 3
    Request receiving bank is contacted

    Time is critical — sooner = higher chance of frozen funds.

  4. 4
    Report to Action Fraud

    actionfraud.police.uk or 0300 123 2040. Get a crime reference.

  5. 5
    If refused — Financial Ombudsman

    FOS is free. Decisions are legally binding on your bank.

Am I eligible?

Check PSR eligibility criteria.

  • ✓You authorised the payment yourself
  • ✓You were deceived into making the transfer
  • ✓UK domestic bank transfer
  • ✓Fraud occurred on or after 7 October 2024
  • ✗Payment made to commit fraud yourself
  • ✗Gross negligence (ignored bank warnings)
  • ✗International transfers — UK domestic only

Not sure? The Financial Ombudsman Service is free and can advise.
