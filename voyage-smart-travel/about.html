Our story

How you travel
matters as much as
where you go.

Voyage Smart Travel was built by people who love exploring the world — and who kept running into the same problem: booking travel shouldn't require choosing between convenience, price, and conscience.

Why we exist

"We built AVA because no traveller should have to spend three hours on six different sites just to find out which flight is the least damaging to the planet."

In 2022, our founding team found themselves frustrated — not with travel itself, but with the friction layered on top of it. Price comparison sites buried eco data. Airlines made offsets a post-checkout afterthought. Booking trains across borders required visiting six separate websites.

VST started as a side project to fix that. Two years later, it's a platform used by over 200,000 travellers across 47 countries — and we're just getting started.

Meet AVA

"AVA isn't a search engine. It's a travel companion that understands what you actually value."

AVA — the AI at the heart of VST — was trained on over 2 million real trip itineraries and connects in real time to 47 travel providers. It understands context: "somewhere warm but not too touristy, under two hours flying" is a perfectly valid search query.

More importantly, AVA gives every option a transparent eco score based on actual emissions data — not marketing claims. If a train is significantly cleaner than the flight, AVA says so, clearly, and makes it just as easy to book.

Our approach to sustainability

"We don't believe in guilt-tripping travellers. We believe in making the better choice the easier choice."

Every flight, hotel, and experience on VST carries a score from 0–100 based on verified emissions data, offset quality, and operator sustainability practices. We partner only with offset providers audited to Gold Standard or VCS certification.

We also surface low-carbon alternatives — trains, ferries, electric transfers — whenever they're genuinely competitive on time and price. We don't hide these options behind a "green tab." They're front and centre, always.

What we stand for

Our values

01

Radical transparency

Every eco score is explained in plain language. We show the methodology, the data source, and the margin of error. No greenwashing, ever.

02

Traveller-first design

No fake urgency. No countdown timers on prices. No dark patterns pushing you toward the option that earns us more commission. The best choice for you is the best choice for us.

03

Accessibility by default

VST is built for every traveller — including those with mobility needs, visual impairments, or cognitive differences. Accessibility is not a feature. It's a foundation.

04

Planet-positive intent

We offset 100% of the carbon footprint of VST's own operations. We're working toward a model where every booking on the platform is net-zero by 2027.

05

Privacy as respect

Your travel data is yours. We don't sell it, we don't profile you for third-party advertising, and we don't store more than we need to deliver the service.

06

Curiosity over caution

The world is worth seeing. We'll never frame travel as something to feel bad about. Our job is to help you do it better — more thoughtfully, more joyfully.
